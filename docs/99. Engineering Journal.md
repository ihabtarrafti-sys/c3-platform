## Engineering Journal

---

# Purpose

This journal records the chronological engineering history of the Geekay Intelligence Platform and its applications.

Unlike Architecture Decision Records (ADRs), which explain *why* a decision was made, this journal records *when* significant engineering milestones occurred and the overall progress of the project.

It serves as the long-term engineering memory of the platform.

## 2026-06-27

### Platform Foundation v0.3.2 Completed

The Platform Foundation phase was completed.

Major accomplishments include:

* Established the Geekay Intelligence Platform monorepo.
* Introduced a reusable Platform SDK.
* Defined and froze the Platform SDK v1.0 contract.
* Implemented the C3 Runtime as a Platform Application.
* Implemented the SharePoint Framework (SPFx) Host.
* Introduced runtime packaging as an independent distributable.
* Established runtime hosting through a host-agnostic architecture.
* Completed the runtime distribution strategy.
* Finalized the build pipeline for all platform packages.
* Introduced PROJECT_STATUS.md and Knowledge Transfer documentation.
* Completed the initial engineering handover package.

At this point the platform architecture was considered stable.

Future development shifted from platform architecture toward platform integration and product delivery.

## Engineering Principles Established

The following principles became permanent platform standards during Platform Foundation:

* Platform before application.
* Host independence.
* Replace implementations rather than rewrite applications.
* Freeze stable contracts.
* Every sprint should produce visible product progress.
* Architecture should enable delivery rather than become the product itself.
