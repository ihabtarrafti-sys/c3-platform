## Cost envelopes

- T1: prepared TTL is `2 × deadline`; the default is `2 × 420s = 14 minutes`, and boot algebra caps configured prepared TTL at the configured lease TTL's two-hour maximum (`webv0/apps/api/src/app.ts:427-450,654-665`). The sweep refuses while the row remains prepared; an owner resolve/arm transition may end that condition earlier, and the remaining TTL is the upper bound on eligibility wait before an operator rerun (`webv0/packages/persistence/src/writeTx.ts:1275-1319`; `webv0/packages/persistence/src/blobBundle.ts:228-251`). This bounded wait is acceptable only because a prepared producer may still own bytes and consuming it would reopen the erasure edge. Because sweep does not auto-resume, wall-clock exit completion also includes operator delay, which is not code-bounded. An expiry between arm and census needs only an immediate rerun (`webv0/packages/persistence/src/blobBundle.ts:228-251`). In the composed post-finalize schedule, R2 cleanup has a fresh 120-second cap and adds no further exit park (`webv0/apps/api/src/app.ts:654-659`; `webv0/apps/api/src/storage.ts:22-26,75-80`; `webv0/apps/api/test/exitUploadSafety.test.ts:267-332`).
- T2: every noncommitted outcome after lease acquisition—including validation/no-byte failures—keeps the publication fence live until the remaining lease TTL; the default is 15 minutes and the configured absolute cap is two hours (`webv0/apps/api/src/app.ts:427-450,1919-1927,2026-2036,2057-2061`; `webv0/packages/persistence/src/exitTenant.ts:163-191`). This bounded condition affects exit only when it overlaps a noncommitted guest attempt and is acceptable because it preserves the fence around indeterminate remote publication. The default per-attempt drain timeout is 60 seconds (caller-configurable); the drain proceeds as soon as no live lease remains and otherwise refuses at that timeout with an operator rerun instruction. Because it does not auto-resume, wall-clock exit completion additionally includes operator delay and is not code-bounded (`webv0/packages/persistence/src/exitTenant.ts:174-190`). A committed claim only attempts early release; release failure also self-heals at TTL (`webv0/apps/api/src/app.ts:2048-2061`).
