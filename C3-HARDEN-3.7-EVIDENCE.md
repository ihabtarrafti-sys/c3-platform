## Cost envelopes

- **J / proposed `JANITOR_WINDOW`: unavailable.** The work order proposes seven days, but no 0078 table, janitor, or bounded scheduler was built. Under the accepted no-maximum provider model, seven days cannot bound byte life; after retirement, a day-8 publication survives (`C3-HARDEN-3.7-ESCALATION.md:7-30`). There is therefore no honest J cost envelope to certify in this wave.
- **U4 checkout:** `createPersistence` defaults pool checkout/connection wait to **10,000 ms**, rejects non-positive/non-safe configured values, and the default is below the supported production request-deadline minimum of **30,000 ms** (`webv0/packages/persistence/src/stores.ts:18-54`; `webv0/apps/api/src/env.ts:61-69`). An already-aborted call refuses before queueing; a signal that fires while queued is rechecked as soon as checkout returns, and a saturated checkout independently fails by 10 seconds (`webv0/packages/persistence/src/tenantContext.ts:34-50`). The zero-bound and real 100 ms tests prove refusal, waiter removal, and later pool reuse (`webv0/packages/persistence/test/transactionDeadline.test.ts:23-27,73-100`). This removes the prior indefinite pool-queue reset surface. It does not claim cancellation after the write callback begins.
- **Prepared-intent availability:** a staff pre-register that legitimately commits before its deadline may still park sweep until its prepared TTL; that TTL is `2 × deadline`, and boot algebra keeps it at or below the two-hour lease cap (`webv0/apps/api/src/app.ts:424-455,676-690`; `webv0/packages/persistence/migrations/0075_intake_lease_ttl_bounds.sql:7-24`). Sweep refuses with a rerun instruction, so operator rerun delay remains outside the code bound (`webv0/packages/persistence/src/blobBundle.ts:228-261`).
- **Guest lease correction:** the default lease park is **15 minutes**, configuration is capped at **2 hours**, and a single exit drain attempt defaults to **60 seconds** before refusing for an operator rerun (`webv0/apps/api/src/app.ts:437-455`; `webv0/packages/persistence/migrations/0075_intake_lease_ttl_bounds.sql:7-24`; `webv0/packages/persistence/src/exitTenant.ts:163-190`). These numbers bound local exit availability only. They do not bound provider publication.
